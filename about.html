Our Story

2009 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to revolutionize bathroom experiences. Started as a small family business with a passion for quality and design.

2013 - First Major Expansion

Opened our first manufacturing facility and expanded our product line to include premium shower systems and smart bathroom solutions.

2019 - Smart Solutions

Launched our smart bathroom collection, integrating IoT technology with traditional craftsmanship for the modern home.

2024 - Global Presence

Today, Waterlife serves customers across 50+ cities with over 500 premium products, maintaining our commitment to excellence and innovation.
