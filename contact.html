Frequently Asked Questions

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all our premium bathroom fittings, covering manufacturing defects and normal wear. Our warranty includes free replacement parts and professional installation support.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes! We have a network of certified installers across all our service areas. Installation services can be scheduled during checkout or by contacting our customer service team. All installations come with a 2-year service guarantee.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days for in-stock items. Custom orders may take 2-3 weeks. We offer expedited shipping options and will provide tracking information once your order ships.

Can I return products if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in original packaging. Custom or installed items may have different return conditions. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer bulk pricing for contractors?

Yes, we have special pricing programs for contractors, builders, and designers. Contact our business development team to learn about volume discounts, trade accounts, and exclusive contractor benefits.
